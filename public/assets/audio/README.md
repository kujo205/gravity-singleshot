# Audio Assets

Place your audio files here:

- `music/` - Background music tracks
- `sfx/` - Sound effects

## Suggested Audio Files

### Music
- `menu-music.mp3` - Menu background music
- `gameplay-music.mp3` - In-game background music

### Sound Effects
- `launch.mp3` - Ball launch sound
- `bounce.mp3` - Collision sound
- `checkpoint.mp3` - Checkpoint reached
- `complete.mp3` - Level complete sound
- `button-click.mp3` - UI button click
